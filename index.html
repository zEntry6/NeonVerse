Our Roadmap

Phase 1 - Conceptualization

Shaping the core vision of NeonVerse, defining utilities, lore, and design aesthetics. Team formation and initial strategy sessions.

Phase 2 - NFT Launch

Release of our Genesis Collection, featuring unique hand-drawn characters. Early supporters get exclusive perks and whitelist spots.

Phase 3 - Community Growth

Hosting giveaways, Discord events, and collaborations with other NFT communities. Building strong and loyal holders.

Phase 4 - Utility & Staking

Launch of staking mechanism, enabling holders to earn tokens. Introduction of NeonPass for exclusive content access.

Phase 5 - Metaverse Integration

Integration of NeonVerse assets into partner metaverse platforms. Begin development of our own immersive virtual space.

Phase 6 - Expansion & Sustainability

Launch of new NFT series, DAO governance, merch store, and sustainable roadmap updates based on community feedback.
